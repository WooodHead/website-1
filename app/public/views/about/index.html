About PS2Alerts

Home to Planetside 2 Alert Metrics

Hi there! Welcome to PS2Alerts!

PS2Alerts was created by Matt Cavanagh , who felt that there was a massive gap in the overall experience in Planetside 2: A large scale metagame.

This website collects information on events called "Alerts" within the game. Such information is gathered and then displayed on this website in a meaningful form. Every kill, death, suicide and teamkill is recorded, along with associated metrics such as Facility Captures, Weapon usage and Vehicle metrics.

All in real-time.

Currently the website is undergoing a re-write, which means features that were on the website are being redeveloped and remade. Stay tuned for info!
